MENU FAVORITES

Image credit to discovertheburgh.com

If you ever end up at Biddle's Escape by accident (or go there specifically because I've written this lovely blog and you are intrigued) be sure to check out these following items from the menu, which are the Writing Girls' favorites.


Arielle's Picks:

Hot Chocolate: 12oz/16oz

  • Steamed milk with cocoa powder: I recommend getting whipped cream on top!

    • Grilled Cheese

  • American, Brie, and Goat cheese. Eat it while it's hot! (May I point out that all of us picked the Grilled Cheese as our primary favorite food at Biddles. I hope that says something *hint hint*)

    • Image credit to Jessica L.


    Shannon's Picks:

    London Fog Tea Latte: 12oz/16oz

  • Vanilla syrup mixed into Earl Grey tea and steamed milk. Warms you right up on those rainy Pittsburgh days.

    • Hummus

  • Chickpea and garlic. Who doesn't love some hummus and pita bread?


    • Paige's Picks:

    Mango Ceylon Tea: 12oz/16oz

  • Mango. Fruity and cheerful.

    • Image credit to Zinat M.


    Katy's Picks:

    Coconut Tea: 12oz/16oz

  • This yummy tea takes you to the tropics.

    • Breakfast Burrito

  • Cheese, beef, peppers, onions. Warm and filling.
